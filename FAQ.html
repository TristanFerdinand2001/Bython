What is Bython?

Bython is a free learning platform that helps people around the world to learn programming in python. We help you learn the basics to the language which are covered in all topics. Once you feel confident about the topics we suggest various resources such as third-party websites, books, IDE's and much more! Bython allows you to take a quiz after every two topics, this allows you to go over the topics multiple times and revise topics you are not too confident in.

Is Bython free?

Bython’s mission is to make python available to everyone. So yes, you can access all of Bython’s Course content completely free! Learners have access to all of our resources such as third-party websites, books, IDE's and much more!

Do I need any existing skills or knowledge to use Bython?

Whether you’re a complete beginner or already have some coding experience, Bython has topics tailored to your level. Our Introduction topics start with foundational concepts and will quickly teach you hands-on, practical skills that you can start using straight away with our suggested IDE's Our topics are great for those who have completed an Introductory Course or those who already have some experience and want to take their skills to the next level.

When are the quizzes taken?

Quizzes are taken after every two topics. If you do not feel comfortable with the two topics, you can look over them again and again until you do. When it comes to programming practice is key. Doing it once wont stick in you head however looking over and trying examples will most definelty help you with your programming journey.

Quizzes in later topics may also include subjects from early topics, this is done as a way to refresh your memory and see if you still have the knowledge. We suggest completing all of the quizzes before moving on to any of our suggested resources.
